Conference Venue Location

San Francisco Convention Center is conveniently located in the heart of downtown San Francisco, with easy access to public transportation, hotels, and dining options.

Transportation and Parking

  • Public Transit: BART Powell Street Station (3 blocks away)
  • Muni Lines: Multiple bus and streetcar lines stop nearby
  • Parking: Convention Center garage available ($25/day)
  • Ride Share: Designated pickup/drop-off zones on Howard Street
  • Airports: 30 minutes from SFO, 45 minutes from Oakland

Frequently Asked Questions

Registration and Tickets

  • Q: When does early bird pricing end?
    A: Early bird pricing is available until July 31, 2024.
  • Q: Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?
    A: Yes, tickets can be transferred up to 48 hours before the event.
  • Q: What is your refund policy?
    A: Full refunds available until August 15, 2024. After that, 50% refunds until September 1.

Event Information

  • Q: Will sessions be recorded?
    A: Yes, all main sessions will be recorded and available to attendees for one year.
  • Q: Is lunch provided?
    A: Yes, lunch is included both days, along with breakfast and refreshments.
  • Q: What should I bring?
    A: Bring a laptop for workshops, business cards for networking, and comfortable shoes!
